--------------------------------------------------------------------------------  
Thick Stir-Fry Noodles (Plain, For Sauce or Stir-Fry Use)  
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------  
  - Cal: ~600 (per batch) (12–15g protein)  
  - Yields: ~2 servings cooked (~300 kcal each)  
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------  
Ingredients:  
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------  
- All-Purpose Flour     - 200g (~1.5 cups)  
- Salt                  - 1/2 tsp  
- Warm Water            - ~100ml (add slowly)  
- Cornstarch (dusting)  - as needed  

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------  
Preparation:  
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------  
1. Mix flour + salt. Slowly add water.  
2. Knead 8–10 mins → firm, elastic dough.  
3. Rest 30 mins covered (room temp).  
4. Roll to 3mm thick sheet.  
5. Cut into wide strips (1–2cm).  
6. Dust with cornstarch, form loose nests.  

Cooking:  
- Boil fresh: 2–4 min (taste for chew)  
- Boil from frozen: 4–6 min  
- Rinse with cold water if stir-frying  

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------  
Storage:  
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------  
- **Raw (best):**  
  - Dust heavily with starch  
  - Freeze on tray, then bag  
  - Cook directly from frozen  

- **Fridge (short-term):**  
  - 1–2 days max, airtight container  
  - May stick, discolor

- **Cooked:**  
  - Toss lightly with oil  
  - Store in airtight container  
  - Up to 3 days refrigerated  

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------  
Caloric Breakdown (est.):  
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------  
Flour (200g): ~682 kcal  
→ Loss during boiling: ~10–15%  
→ Final yield: ~600 kcal  
→ Protein: ~12–15g
