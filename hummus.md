--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Hummus (Soybean or Chickpea)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Variants: Soy or Chickpea | w/ or w/o Tahini
- Soak Time: Soybeans 8–12hr, Chickpeas 8–12hr
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Ingredients:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Soybeans or Chickpeas (dry) - 100g (~1/2 cup) → Soaked: ~220g (~1 cup)
- Water                       - as needed
- Salt                        - to taste
- Lemon Juice                 - 2 tbsp
- Garlic                      - 2 cloves minced
- Cumin Powder                - 1 tsp 
- Olive Oil                   - 1 tbsp (13.5g)
- Tahini (optional)           - 1 tbsp (15g)
- Paprika                     - 1/2 tsp
- Black Pepper                - 1/4 tsp
- Aquafaba (optional)         - 2 tbsp (from cooked beans)
- Cilantro/Parsley            - chopped, for garnish
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Preparation:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Rinse, soak beans 8–12hr.

2. Cook beans:
   - Pressure Cook: 15 PSI for 18–22 min, natural release.
   - Stovetop Simmer: 90 min until soft.

3. Blend hummus:
   - Blend beans with garlic, lemon, cumin, salt, olive oil.
   - Add tahini if using. Add aquafaba if smoother texture is needed.

4. Adjust seasoning, garnish with paprika, black pepper, herbs.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Caloric Breakdown:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- 1 cup cooked chickpeas ≈ 269 kcal, 14.5 g protein  
- Olive oil (1 tbsp) ≈ 120 kcal, 0 g protein  
- Tahini (1 tbsp, opt) ≈ 90 kcal, 2.6 g protein  
- Lemon juice (2 tbsp) ≈ 8 kcal, ~0 g protein  
- Garlic (2 cloves) ≈ 8 kcal, 0.3 g protein  
- Cumin powder (1 tsp) ≈ 8 kcal, 0.4 g protein  
- Paprika (1/2 tsp) ≈ 4 kcal, ~0 g protein  
- Black pepper (1/4 tsp) ≈ 2 kcal, ~0 g protein  
- Aquafaba, herbs, salt, water ≈ 0 kcal  

	- Soybeans add ~120 kcal, ~18g more protein than chickpeas.
	- Use soy for higher protein; chickpeas for traditional flavor.
	
TOTAL (w/ tahini):  
~509 kcal, ~17.8 g protein  

TOTAL (no tahini):  
~419 kcal, ~15.2 g protein
