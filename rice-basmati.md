--------------------------------------------------------------------------------  
Basmati Rice (Simple, Whole Food, Vegan)  
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------  
  - Cal: ~520 (150g dry rice ~= 3/4 cup)  
  - Yield: ~2.25 cups cooked  
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------  
Ingredients:  
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------  
- Basmati Rice (dry)  - 150g (~3/4 cup)  
- Water              - 2.25 cups  
- Salt               - to taste (optional)  

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------  
Preparation:  
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------  
1. Rinse rice 2-3 times until water runs clear.  

2. Soak rice in water for 20-30 min, then drain (optional for fluffier rice).  

3. Bring 2.25 cups water to boil in a pot.  

4. Add rice, stir once, cover with lid.  

5. Reduce heat to low, simmer 12-15 min until water is absorbed.  

6. Remove from heat, let rest 5 min covered.  

7. Fluff with fork, serve.  
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------  
Caloric Breakdown for Basmati Rice  
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------  
Basmati Rice (dry, 150g) : 520 kcal (3.47 kcal/g)  
Total                    : ~520 kcal
