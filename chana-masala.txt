--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Chana Masala (Bright Red, Restaurant Style, Vegan, 1 Serving)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  - Cal: ~800
  - Protein: ~26g
  - Soak Time: Chana (8–12 hr if using dry)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Ingredients:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Chickpeas (cooked)     - (1.5 cup / 250g) or (120g /0.75 cup) dry → soak + cook
- Tomato Sauce           - 1 cup (see recipe below or use canned puree)
- Onion (fine)           - 1 small (~1/2 cup)
- Garlic (minced)        - 2 cloves
- Ginger (minced)        - 1 tsp
- Cumin Seeds            - 1/2 tsp
- Bay Leaf               - 1
- Clove, Cardamom, Cin.  - 1 each (opt)
- Coriander Powder       - 1/2 tsp
- Kashmiri Chili Powder  - 1/2 tsp (or paprika + cayenne pinch)
- Turmeric Powder        - 1/8 tsp
- Garam Masala           - 1/8 tsp (max)
- Salt                   - to taste (~1/2 tsp)
- Sugar                  - pinch (opt)
- Oil                    - 2 tbsp
- Kasuri Methi (opt)     - 1 tsp, crushed
- Lemon Juice / Amchur   - 1/2 tsp / 1/4 tsp
- Cilantro               - garnish
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Instructions:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Cook chana till soft (pressure: 15–20 min @ 15 psi, natural release).
2. Heat oil, splutter cumin. Add bay + whole spices (opt).
3. Add onion + salt. Sauté soft (no browning).
4. Add garlic, ginger. Sauté 1 min.
5. Add coriander, chili, turmeric, garam. Stir 30 sec.
6. Add tomato sauce. Simmer 10 min, thicken.
7. Add chana + water if thick. Add salt + sugar.
8. Simmer 10–15 min. Mash a few chana to thicken.
9. Add kasuri methi + lemon/amchur. Remove whole spices.
10. Garnish cilantro, optional vegan butter. Serve hot.
