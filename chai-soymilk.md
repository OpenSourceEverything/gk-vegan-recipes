--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Soymilk Chai Tea (Fresh-Spice, 1 Large Mug)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  - Yield: ~12-14 oz
  - Notes: whole spices give cleaner flavor; ground works in a pinch.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Ingredients:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Water                    - 1/2 cup (120 ml)
- Soy Milk                 - 1 1/2 cups (360 ml)
- Black Tea (optional)     - 1-2 tsp loose or 1-2 bags
- Star Anise               - 1 pod (minimum)
- Cinnamon Stick           - 1 small
- Cardamom Pods (green)    - 2-3, cracked
- Cloves                   - 2
- Black Peppercorns        - 4-6
- Fresh Ginger             - 3-4 slices
- Sweetener                - 1-2 tsp (optional)
- Vanilla Extract          - 1/4 tsp (optional)
- Salt                     - tiny pinch (optional)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Instructions:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Lightly crush the whole spices to open them up.
2. Simmer water, spices, and ginger for 5-7 minutes.
3. Add soy milk and keep at a gentle simmer (do not hard boil).
4. Add tea and steep 3-4 minutes, then remove the tea.
5. Strain into a mug and sweeten to taste.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Ground-Spice Option (if no whole spices)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Replace whole spices with: 1/8 tsp cinnamon, 1/8 tsp cardamom, 1/8 tsp ginger,
  1/16 tsp clove, 1/16 tsp black pepper, and a small pinch of star anise powder.
- Simmer in the water first, then strain through a fine mesh before adding tea.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Nutrition (approx per mug)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Calories: ~120-170 (depends on soy milk + sweetener)
- Protein: ~8-11g (depends on soy milk)
