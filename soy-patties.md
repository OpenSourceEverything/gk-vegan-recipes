--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Soy Patties (~4–6 Patties)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  - Cal: ~860
  - Protein: ~56g
  - Base: Whole soybeans
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Ingredients:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Dry Soybeans                 - 1     cup  (170g dry) → 
                               - 2.5-3 cup  (~450 soaked)
                               - ~620 cal, ~55g protein  
- Water (for blend)            - 3/4 cup (~180ml)
- Soy Sauce                    - 1 tbsp (~10g) → ~10 cal, ~1g protein  
- Tomato Paste                 - 1 tbsp (~15g) → ~15 cal, ~0.5g protein  
- Onion Powder                 - 1 tsp (~2.4g) → ~8 cal  
- Garlic Powder                - 1 tsp (~2.8g) → ~9 cal  
- Paprika                      - 1/2 tsp (~1g) → ~3 cal  
- Beet Powder                  - 1 tbsp (~6g) → ~20 cal  
- Refined Coconut Oil          - 2 tbsp (28g) → ~240 cal  
- Flaxseed Meal                - 2 tbsp (14g) + 6 tbsp water → ~75 cal, ~2.5g protein  
- Tapioca/Potato Starch        - 2 tbsp (16g) → ~60 cal  

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Instructions:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Soak soybeans 12 hr.  
2. Blend with 3/4 cup water to thick paste.  
3. Cook paste on low heat ~15 min, stir often.  
4. Let cool 10 min.  
5. Mix in all remaining ingredients.  
6. Chill 1 hr to firm.  
7. Form into patties, logs, or balls.  
8. Bake at 350°F (175°C) for ~25 min, flip halfway.  
9. Optional: pan-sear for crust.  

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Nutrition Breakdown (1 Batch ~4–6 Patties)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Soybeans (170g dry)              → ~620 cal, ~55g protein  
- Soy Sauce + Tomato Paste        → ~25 cal, ~1.5g protein  
- Spices + Beet Powder (~12g)     → ~40 cal  
- Coconut Oil (28g)               → ~240 cal  
- Flax + Starch (30g)             → ~135 cal, ~2.5g protein  
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Total                             → ~860 cal, ~59g protein
