basic non‑tofu seitan vwg strips recipe:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
VWG Strips (No Tofu, High‑Protein, ~156 cal/strip)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Makes ~6 strips
- Per strip: ~156 cal, ~26 g protein, ~3 g fat
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Ingredients:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Vital wheat gluten     - 1½ cups (≈180 g)
- Water                  - ¾ cup
- Neutral oil            - 1 Tbsp
- Chickpea flour         - 2 Tbsp
- Nutritional yeast      - 2 Tbsp
- Onion powder           - 1 Tbsp
- Garlic powder          - 2 tsp
- Vegetable broth powder - 2 tsp (or 1 bouillon cube)
- Salt                   - ½ tsp

optional breading:
- Plant milk + flour or breadcrumbs

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Instructions:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
0. Prep
1. Mix dry ingredients in bowl.
2. Whisk water + oil. Add to dry mix. Stir to form dough.
3. Knead ~1 min until cohesive.
4. Shape into log, wrap in foil or parchment.
5. Steam or simmer 25 min; rest 1 h.
6. Slice into strips (~6).
7. Optional breading: dip in plant milk, coat in flour/breadcrumbs.
8. Pan‑fry or bake until golden/crispy.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Nutrition Summary:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- ~156 cal • ~26 g protein • ~3 g fat • ~8 g carbs per strip
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
notes:
- chickpea flour aids tenderness, prevents rubbery texture
- oil improves mouthfeel
- simple, reliable non‑tofu seitan
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
