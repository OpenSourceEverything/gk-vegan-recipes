--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Falafel (Flexible Base, Pan or Oven, 1 Batch ~8 Patties)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  - Cal: ~728
  - Protein: ~23g
  - Base: Okara, mashed squash, sweet potato, lentils
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Ingredients:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Base (e.g. okara)           - 1 cup (~240g okara or ~200g mash)  
  - if Sweet Potato           - 1/2 or 1/4 cup!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!
- Mixed Veggies (chopped)     - 1 cup (~100g)
- Whole Wheat Flour           - 1 cup (~120g)
- Baking Powder               - 1/2 tsp
- Fresh Parsley (chopped)     - 1/4 cup (~10g)
- Garlic (minced)             - 1–2 cloves
- Ground Cumin                - 1 tsp
- Paprika                     - 1/2 tsp
- Salt + Pepper               - to taste
- Water                       - 2–3 tbsp (if needed)
- Oil                         - 1 tbsp (for pan) or spray (for oven)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Instructions:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. In bowl, mix base, veggies, parsley, garlic.
2. Add flour, baking powder, cumin, paprika, salt, pepper. Combine well.
3. If dry, add water gradually to hold shape.
4. Form patties or balls.
5. Pan-fry in oil till golden both sides,
   or bake at 375°F (190°C) ~20–25 min, flip halfway.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Nutrition Breakdown by Ingredient (1 Batch ~8 Patties)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Base (okara, 240g)              → ~150 cal, ~12g protein  
- Veggies (100g)                 → ~40 cal, ~2g protein  
- Whole Wheat Flour (120g)       → ~400 cal, ~13g protein  
- Garlic + Spices + Herbs (~15g) → ~10 cal, ~0.5g protein  
- Oil (1 tbsp, 14g)              → ~120 cal, 0g protein  
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Total                            → ~720 cal, ~27.5g protein
