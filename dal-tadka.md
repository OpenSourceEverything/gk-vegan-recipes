--------------------------------------------------------------------------------  
Dal Tadka (North Indian Style)  
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------  
  - Cal: ~600  (437 dal) (32g protein)
  - Soak Times: Rajma/Urad: 8-12hr | Chana: 1-4hr | Moong: 1hr  
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------  
Ingredients:  
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------  
- Urad Dal (dry)  - 35g (~2 tbsp) → Soaked: 70g (~1/3 cup)  
- Rajma (dry)     - 35g (~2 tbsp) → Soaked: 90g (~1/2 cup)  
- Chana Dal (dry) - 35g (~2 tbsp) → Soaked: 70g (~1/3 cup)  
- Moong Dal (dry) - 20g (~1.5 tbsp) → Soaked: 40g (~3 tbsp)  
- Water           - 3.5 cups  
- Salt            - to taste  
- Cilantro        - chopped, for garnish  

Saute:  
- Butter/oil 		- 2 tsp  
- Cumin Seeds 		- 3/4 tsp  
- Garlic 		- 3 cloves minced  
- Ginger 		- 3/4 tsp grated  
- Green Chili 		- 1 chopped  
- Onion 		- 1 small (~1/3 cup chopped)  
- Tomato 		- 1 small (~1/3 cup chopped)  
- Turmeric 		- 1/3 tsp  
- Red Chili Powder 	- 1/3 tsp  
- Garam Masala 		- 1/3 tsp  

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------  
Preparation:  
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------  
1. Rinse, soak dals/rajma as per times above.  

2. Pressure cook dals + rajma  
  - Oil: 1 tbsp per cup to reduce frothing.  
  - 15 PSI: 12-15 min after first hiss, natural release.  
  - 10 PSI: 18-20 min after first hiss, natural release.  
  - 5 PSI: 30-35 min after first hiss, natural release.  

3. Sauté:  
  - Heat ghee, splutter cumin. Add garlic, ginger, chili, sauté.  
  - Add onion, cook golden. Add tomato, spices, cook soft.  

4. Mix tadka into dal, simmer 5 min, garnish with cilantro.
