--------------------------------------------------------------------------------  
Moong Dal Vegan Scramble (Revised Nutrition)  
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------  
  - Calories: ~370  
  - Protein: ~29g  
  - Soak Time: 8 hr, 1hr min for split, 4hr for whole
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------  
Ingredients:  
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------  
- Moong Dal               - 100g  (~0.56 cups) dry 
                          - ~220g (~1.24 cups) soaked 
- Water                   - 1/2 cup (adjust for texture)  
- Salt                    - to taste  
- Baking Powder           - 1/4 tsp  
- Kala Namak (black salt) - 1/4 tsp  
- Turmeric                - 1/8 tsp  
- Garlic Powder           - 1/4 tsp (optional)  
- Nutritional Yeast       - 1 tbsp (~5g)  
- Oil Spray / Light Oil   - for pan only  
- Optional Veg (minced): bell pepper, onion, spinach  

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------  
Preparation:  
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------  
1. Soak moong dal 8 hr. Drain.  

2. Blend soaked dal with water until smooth batter.  

3. Add salt, kala namak, turmeric, garlic powder, baking powder,  
   nutritional yeast. Blend briefly.  

4. Heat pan medium-low. Lightly oil or spray.  

5. Pour batter, stir like scrambled eggs for 6–8 min until set.  

6. Add veg in last 2–3 min if using. Serve hot.  

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------  
Caloric + Protein Breakdown  
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------  
Moong Dal (100g dry)       : 347 kcal, 24g protein  
Nutritional Yeast (5g)     : 17 kcal, 2.6g protein  
Kala Namak, Turmeric, Spices: ~5 kcal, 0g protein  
Baking Powder (1/4 tsp)    : ~1 kcal, 0g protein  
Oil Spray                  : ~10 kcal (estimate)  

Optional Veg (25g total)   : ~10–20 kcal, 1–2g protein  

Total                      : ~370–380 kcal  
Protein                    : ~27–29g
