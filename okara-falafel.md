--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Okara Falafel (High-Protein, 1 Batch ~8 Patties)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  - Cal: ~540 (pan-fry with 1 tbsp oil) or ~430 (bake with 1 tsp oil)
  - Protein: ~43g
  - Base: 1 cup okara + 1/4 cup VWG + 1/4 cup chickpea flour
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Ingredients:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Okara (pressed)          - 1 cup (~240g)
- Vital Wheat Gluten       - 1/4 cup (~30g)
- Chickpea Flour           - 1/4 cup (~30g)
- Onion (fine)             - 1/2 cup (~80g)
- Garlic (minced)          - 2 cloves
- Parsley/Cilantro (chop)  - 1/4 cup (~10g)
- Lemon Juice              - 1 tbsp
- Ground Cumin             - 1 tsp
- Ground Coriander         - 1 tsp
- Black Pepper (ground)    - 1 tsp
- Baking Powder            - 1/2 tsp
- Salt                     - 1/2 tsp (to taste)
- Water                    - 1-3 tbsp (as needed)
- Oil                      - 1 tbsp for pan, or 1 tsp for baking

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Instructions:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Press okara to remove excess water and fluff.
2. Mix okara, onion, garlic, herbs, and lemon juice.
3. Add VWG, chickpea flour, spices, baking powder, and salt. Mix until cohesive.
4. If dry, add water 1 tbsp at a time. Dough should hold shape without sticking.
5. Rest 5 minutes so VWG hydrates. Form 8 patties.
6. Pan: cook in oil 2-3 min per side until golden.
7. Oven: brush or spray lightly, bake at 400F / 205C for 18-22 min, flip halfway.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Nutrition Breakdown by Ingredient (1 Batch)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Okara (240g)                - ~150 cal, ~12g protein
- Vital Wheat Gluten (30g)    - ~110 cal, ~23g protein
- Chickpea Flour (30g)        - ~110 cal, ~6g protein
- Onion (80g)                 - ~30 cal, ~1g protein
- Garlic (6g)                 - ~10 cal, ~0.5g protein
- Herbs + Lemon + Spices      - ~10 cal, ~0.5g protein
- Oil (1 tbsp, 14g)           - ~120 cal, 0g protein
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Total                         - ~540 cal, ~43g protein
Note: baked version with 1 tsp oil is ~430 cal.
