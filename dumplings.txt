--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Steamed Dumplings (Whole Wheat, Mixed Veggies)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Cal: ~680 kcal per batch (~20 dumplings)
- Protein: ~20g per batch
- Cooking Time: Steam 10–12 min
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Ingredients:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Whole Wheat Flour       - 150g (~1 cup)  
- Mixed Veggies (chopped) - 250g (~2 cups)  
  (e.g. cabbage, carrot, onion, spinach)  
- Garlic                  - 2 cloves minced  
- Ginger                  - 1 tsp minced  
- Soy Sauce               - 1 tbsp  
- Sesame Oil              - 1 tsp  
- Salt                    - to taste  
- Water                   - ~70ml (for dough)  
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Preparation:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Mix flour with water + pinch of salt to form soft dough. Rest 20 min.

2. In pan: sauté garlic, ginger, veggies, soy sauce, sesame oil.

3. Roll dough into small rounds (~3 in diameter), add 1 tbsp filling.

4. Fold/seal dumplings. Steam 10–12 min until dough translucent.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Notes:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Add tofu/okara to boost protein.
- Can pan-fry after steaming for texture.
- Serve with soy-vinegar dip.
