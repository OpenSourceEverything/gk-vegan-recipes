North Indian Garam Masala  

1. Coriander seeds - 2 tbsp  
2. Cumin seeds - 1 tbsp  
3. Black peppercorns - 1 tbsp  
4. Green cardamom - 6 pods  
5. Black cardamom - 2 pods  
6. Cloves - 1 tsp  
7. Cinnamon - 2-inch stick  
8. Bay leaves - 2  
9. Nutmeg - 1/4 piece  
10. Mace - 2 strands  

Toast spices until aromatic. Cool, grind to fine powder. Store airtight.
