--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Name
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Soy Protein Isolate Shakes

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Summary
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
cacao shake with soy protein isolate and 0 cal sweetener.
Batch fills 48oz blender to brim. Recipe produces 4 12 oz shakes each with 
50g protein and approximately
Formulated using SPI label values: 110 kcal and 25 g protein per 32 g SPI.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Yield
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Yield: 4 servings
- Per serving: ~12 oz (~360 g)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Assumed SPI Label (Explicit)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- 32 g SPI = 110 kcal, 25 g protein
- This recipe targets ~27 g protein per shake, not 40 g

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Ingredients:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Ingredient            | Batch (4 shakes)        | Per Shake
---------------------|-------------------------|-------------------
Water                | 700-800 mL/g            | 
Soy protein isolate  | 2 cups                  | 0.25 cup
Erythritol           | 28 g                    | 7 g
Cocoa powder         | 20 g                    | 5 g
Salt                 | ~1/4 tsp                | pinch
Xanthan gum          | 0.8 g                   | 0.2 g
Sucralose (pure)     | 40-60 mg                | 10-15 mg
EVOO                 | 10 g                    | 2.5 g

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Steps:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
0. Dry-mix SPI, erythritol, cocoa, salt, xanthan.
1. Add water to blender.
2. Add dry mix; blend 20-30 seconds until smooth.
3. Add EVOO; pulse briefly.
4. Portion into 4 servings (~12 oz each).

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Nutrition (Approx Per Serving)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Calories: ~150-160 kcal
- Protein: ~26-27 g
- Fat: ~2.5 g
- Net carbs: ~0 g
