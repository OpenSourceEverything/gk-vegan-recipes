--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Name
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Low Sat Fat Maltodextrin Nice Cream

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Summary
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Frozen fruit nice cream using maltodextrin for body, very low saturated fat,
low added sugar, blender-only.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Yield
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Yield: 1 large serving (~450 g total)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Preparation
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Freeze fruit solid.
- Measure dry ingredients ahead.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Tools
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- High power blender
- Tamper or spatula
- Kitchen scale

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Ingredients:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Ingredient                     | kcal / dose | Notes
-------------------------------|-------------|---------------------------
240 g frozen banana            | ~215 kcal   | Texture + sweetness
120 g frozen strawberries      | ~38 kcal    | Flavor, low sugar
120 ml unsweetened soy milk    | ~40 kcal    | Low sat fat liquid
20 g maltodextrin (DE 10-19)   | ~80 kcal    | Bulk, creaminess
5 g allulose or erythritol     | ~0 kcal     | Optional
1 g xanthan gum                | ~5 kcal     | Anti-ice, structure
1 ml vanilla extract           | ~2 kcal     | Optional
Pinch salt                     | 0 kcal      | Flavor balance

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Steps:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
0. Add soy milk to blender.
1. Add frozen fruit.
2. Add maltodextrin, sweetener, xanthan, salt, vanilla.
3. Blend low, then high, using tamper.
4. Stop once thick and smooth.
5. Serve immediately or freeze 20-30 min to firm.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Notes / Variants:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Cocoa: add 5 g dutch cocoa powder (+20 kcal).
- Higher fiber: replace xanthan with 2-3 g psyllium powder.
- Less sweet: reduce banana to 180 g, add ice.
- Storage: best fresh; refreeze once only, expect iciness.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Nutrition (approx per serving):
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Calories: ~380 kcal
- Protein: ~6 g
- Carbs: ~85 g
- Fat: ~3 g
- Saturated fat: ~0.3-0.5 g
- Key contributor: calories mainly from banana + maltodextrin
