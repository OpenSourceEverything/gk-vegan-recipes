--------------------------------------------------------------------------------  
Quick Roma Tomato Sauce (Vegan, Smooth, Rich Red)  
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------  
  - Yield: ~2 cups  
  - Use: 1 cup per chana masala serving  
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------  
Ingredients:  
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------  
- Roma Tomatoes          - 5–6 (500g)  
- Garlic (minced)        - 2 cloves  
- Ginger (minced)        - 1 tsp (opt)  
- Oil or Water           - 1 tbsp (or splash water)  
- Salt                   - 1/4 tsp  
- Sugar                  - 1/2 tsp  
- Kashmiri Chili Powder  - 1/2 tsp (opt, red color)  
- Bay Leaf               - 1 (opt, remove)  
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------  
Instructions:  
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------  
1. (Opt) Score, blanch, peel tomatoes.  
2. Chop. Sauté garlic/ginger in oil 30 sec.  
3. Add tomatoes, salt, sugar, chili, bay leaf.  
4. Cover, simmer 10 min. Stir occasionally.  
5. Uncover, mash. Simmer 10 min till thick.  
6. Blend smooth. (Strain if needed).  
7. Return to pan. Adjust salt, sugar. Simmer 2 min.  
8. Cool, store. Fridge 4–5 days. Freeze 2–3 mo.  
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
