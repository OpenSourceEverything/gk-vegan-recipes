--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Chocolate Soy Protein Bars (Vegan, Chewy, ~180 Cal, ~20g Protein Each)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Yield: 4 bars
- Per Bar: ~180-190 cal, ~20g protein, ~3g fat
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Ingredients:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Soy Protein Isolate        - 120 g (~445 cal, ~102 g protein)
- Cocoa Powder (unsweet)    - 2 tbsp (~20 g, ~40 cal)
- Erythritol (powdered)     - 30 g (~0 cal)
- Vegetable Glycerin (VG)   - 40 g (~170 cal)
- Water                     - 1-2 tbsp (15-30 g, as needed)
- Sunflower Lecithin        - 1 tsp (~3 g, ~25 cal)
- EVOO (or neutral oil)     - 1.5 tsp (~6 g, ~50 cal)
- Sucralose (pure)          - 20-30 mg (to taste)
- Vanilla Extract           - 1/2 tsp (optional)
- Salt                      - pinch
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Instructions:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
It is recommended to do this with spatula and bowl, not blender.

1. Mix dry ingredients: protein, cocoa, erythritol, salt.
2. Add glycerin, oil, lecithin, vanilla, and sucralose.
3. Mix until a thick paste forms.
4. Add water slowly, 1 tsp at a time, until dough is pliable.
   Target texture: clay-like, bends without cracking.
5. Knead by hand 1-2 minutes to fully hydrate protein.
6. Press firmly into lined tray or bar mold.
7. Chill 60 minutes to set. Slice into 4 bars. Store cold.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Tips:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- If crumbly: add 5 g glycerin.
- If sticky: add 5 g soy protein isolate or erythritol.
- For firmer bars: reduce glycerin by 5 g.
- For extra chew: replace water with additional glycerin.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Macros (Per Bar x4, Approx):
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Calories: ~180-190
- Protein: ~20-22 g
- Fat: ~3 g
- Sugar: ~0 g
- Key contributors: SPI for protein, glycerin for chew,
  erythritol + sucralose for sweetness without sugar.
