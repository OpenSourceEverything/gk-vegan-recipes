--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Impossible-Lite Style Mock Meat (1 Batch)  
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------  
- Cal: ~546  
- Protein: ~63 g  
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------  
Ingredients:  
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------  
- Textured veg protein (dry)     - 105 g  
- Vital wheat gluten             - 10 g  
- Vegetable oil (sunflower)      - 10 g  
- Molasses                       - 20 g  
- Beet powder                     - 10 g  
- Beef base (vegan)               - 2 g  
- Marmite                         - 2 g  
- Methylcellulose (~2% total wt)  - 3.6 g  
- Tomato paste (optional)         - 23 g  
- Garlic powder                   - 2 g (~1/2 tsp)  
- Onion powder                    - 4 g (~1 tsp)  
- Apple cider vinegar             - 5 g (~1 tsp)  
- Water (hydration)               - ~100 g  

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------  
Instructions:  
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------  
1. Hydrate TVP in ~100 g hot water (80–90°C) for 5–10 min.  
2. In small bowl, whisk methylcellulose into cold water (~10× its weight).  
   *Cold mix prevents clumping. Binding forms on heating later.*  
3. Drain excess water from TVP.  
4. Add gluten, beet powder, garlic, onion, beef base, marmite, molasses,  
   vinegar, optional tomato paste. Mix well.  
5. Add oil. Mix until uniform.  
6. Add methylcellulose gel. Knead until cohesive, 2–3 min.  
7. Shape into patties or blocks.  
8. Rest 10–15 min (hydration + gluten development).  
9. Cook on hot skillet/grill until internal temp ≥74°C, firm texture.  

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------  
Nutrition (approx):  
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------  
- Calories: ~546  
- Protein: ~63 g
